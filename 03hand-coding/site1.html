Gameboy Pocket

The Gameboy Pocket was the successor to the Gameboy. The Pocket, had all of the features of its predecessor, but in a smaller form factor. The screen was improved to a true black and white display, eliminating the undesirable coloration of the original Gameboy. Another notable improvement was the batteries, the Pocket required two AAA batteries, rather than four AA.

Release Dates:
Japan: July 21st, 1996.
North America: September 3rd, 1996.

Predecessor: Gameboy

Successor: Gameboy Color
