Gameboy

The Gameboy, a revolutionary handheld device that changed the way we consumed video games. What sets the Gameboy apart from its competitors at the time, is its ability to use cartridges to play games. Meaning if you had a gameboy, you could play multiple games, where as other handheld systems only came with the game loaded into its hard drive.

Release Dates:
Japan: April 21st, 1989.
North America: July 31st, 1989.
Europe: September 28th, 1990.

Successor: Gameboy Pocket
