Gameboy Advance

The Gameboy Advance differed from the past models in two distinct ways. The first and most notable would be the landscape form factor. The second feature that set it apart from previous gameboys was the backlit screen. Gone were the days of needing to plug a light into the system, the backlit meant you could play anytime, day or night without hinderance.

Release Dates:
Japan: March 21st, 2001.
North America: June 11th, 2001.

Predecessor: Gameboy Color

Successor: Gameboy Advance SP
